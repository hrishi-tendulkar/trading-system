# DESIGN

## Purpose

Design in Trading System exists to help the user make better decisions faster. Interfaces and reports should compress dense evidence into a calm, legible weekly workflow rather than imitate consumer trading apps or information-heavy terminals.

## Design Principles

- Decision clarity over dashboard novelty
- Scanability over ornament
- Action labels over ambiguous summaries
- Visual separation of long-term, short-term, and options views
- Evidence traceability over persuasive language
- Calm presentation that respects limited weekday attention

## UX Priorities

- Weekly review flow first
- Daily change digest second
- Holding review with thesis continuity
- Explicit risk and invalidation visibility

## Non-Goals

- Entertainment-style market interfaces
- Dense charting for its own sake
- Hiding uncertainty behind confident visuals
