# PRODUCT

## Purpose

Trading System is a single-user decision-support product for weekly equity intelligence. Its job is to help one investor decide what to buy now, what to buy only on pullback, what to hold or trim, and when covered calls or cash-secured puts are the better expression.

## Target User

- One technically capable investor
- Roughly `$200,000` of short-term capital under active management
- Limited weekday time
- Wants concise, action-oriented weekly output rather than a research sprawl

## Product Principles

- Actionability over volume
- Explainability over opaque scoring
- Weekly consistency over constant activity
- Traceability over unsupported AI commentary
- Feasible v1 scope over overbuilt automation
- Stock-first decisions, options-second overlays
- Glossary-on-demand for non-jargon-first usability

## Core Outcomes

- Surface the best weekly opportunities from a curated universe
- Keep long-term conviction separate from short-term timing
- Preserve historical scores, evidence, and outcomes
- Reduce emotionally reactive decision-making
- Make research review faster and more disciplined
- Let the user inspect market posture, stock-level evidence, and decision-basis logic without needing to already know trading shorthand

## Non-Goals

- Automated order execution
- Intraday trading workflows
- Multi-user collaboration in v1
- Complex options strategies beyond covered calls and cash-secured puts
- Black-box recommendations without evidence
